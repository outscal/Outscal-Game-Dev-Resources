# Yashaswi Shivanand

**Co-Founder · Brewed Games**

> An OG e-sports player from 2003 is now building his dream game studio.

---

IPL - Indian Pro League was one of the first e-sports leagues started by gaming enthusiasts in 2003.

Yashaswi was one of the moderators of this early e-sports community. They started by hosting their first tournament which was sponsored by AMD and had a winning prize of Rs. 40,000/-.

They looked up to @Cyber Athlete Professional League which was a major e-sports league in the USA back then.

But apparently, things went downhill and it didn’t work as they assumed.

Yashaswi started exploring his interests in animation and found out about the Asian Institute of Design from a friend. He went to check it out and was blown by looking at the equipment around.

”I had never seen a double monitor setup before that, people around were sketching, and the creative aura around the place just left me awestruck”

He pursued a diploma in Game Art from there and joined his first full-time job at Atman Software. Atman’s founder had a strong background in game programming and was building his thirst game engine back then. He spent 5 years working at Atman and then switched to Mayaxis which later got branded Xarpie Labs.

Yash recalls how he was sitting in a co-working space in Bangalore and a random person saw him working. He approached and asked Yash to check out their office and the conversation later turned into a job opportunity.

The company had a VR studio for five and a half years where they worked on VR and AR using game engines but not for games.

He really wanted to start building games and fulfill the dream he had always seen.

Stars aligned as parallelly Mahesh Rajamani, Naveen Bhagavan, and Prashanth DG were also ready to start their dream venture.

They are building Brewed Games now and developing Winds of Arcana (An Action RPG).
