# Avichal Singh

**Co - Founder and Game Director · Nodding Head Games**

Escaping from home, escaping from tuition for the love of games.

Featuring Avichal, Founder of Nodding Head!

Story of how a boy who used to cycle around the city to hunt gaming cafes built a game that won 30+ awards.

Avichal’s journey started in 2010 when he heard about a job role called ‘Game Design’.

Out of curiosity, he started googling about animation and everything about it.

“I stumbled upon this college DSK Institute of IT for animation, video games, and industrial design. And I was like, what? Game design? What does that mean?

Even without reading about it, I just knew it's got design and games. That just means it's just a calling like it's happening.”

Everyone around Avichal was highly skeptical about him taking this program instead of going for a regular college degree.

He started pursuing the 4-year course that helped him learn new skills, network, and interact with more folks like him.

Avichal was graduating from college when Shruti and Ian joined as lecturers on the same campus.

The first seven-member team which created Raji’s trailer at nodding heads came from DSK.

“Back in 2013, I was very sure that I wanted to get into a PC, Console internship but couldn’t find one in India. I used to look for international. So when I used to look at some profiles, my dream internship was Creative Assembly back then. I even got a response from them, but couldn’t join because they were looking for a candidate in the UK.”

Nodding Head’s Raji put India on the game dev map. It was the founders’ dream project and they had gone all hands to build that one game. Before Nodding head, Avichal had worked in Zynga where he was working on Farmville, Empires, and Allies for a couple of years. As a designer, he used to read about the pain points of a lot of players, what are they focusing on? And then, he used to make features that he used to take to the team.

“After spending a year at Farmville, I moved to Empires and Allies, and that's where I did some level design, I was working with Jeff, who was from SF Zynga. He had already made 1500+ levels and the challenge was to make more levels with the same elements and Jeff was gonna play them. It improved my level design game and I managed to pull off the challenge. ”

He had two paths in front of his eyes. a) Stay at Zynga and climb the ladder. b) Put down his papers at Zynga, and start his studio.

He chose the latter one!

“The first time I played Bastion and found out it's made by a seven-member team, that thought just put a spell on me. and stayed with me forever in Zynga”

Avichal started building Nodding heads with Shruti and Ian in 2017.

They developed Raji for Switch, PC, Xbox, and PlayStation, and launched all of these within six weeks with a small team.

Nodding heads have managed to achieve what the biggest studios across the globe have failed in.

The immense passion for games that the founding team holds in their eyes gets reflected in their work.

Avichal is evidence of how someone can build a rewarding and satisfying career in games. The hardship never left his side but his perseverance, and constant desire never let his morale down.

Cheers to Avichal, Raji, and team Nodding head!
