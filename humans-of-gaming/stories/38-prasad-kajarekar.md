# Prasad Kajarekar

**Product Lead - Web3 & Games · Loco**

> Two school kids studying in the 9th standard, living in the city of dreams were fascinated by an open-world game and dreamt of building a similar game one day.

---

Two school kids studying in the 9th standard, living in the city of dreams were fascinated by an open-world game and dreamt of building a similar game one day. Ft. Prasad Kajarekar

The two kids were Prasad and Nikhil Malankar and the game they played was GTA Vice City,

They say ‘‘Dream with your eyes open wide’’

Prasad started building GameEon Studios from his college campus. The first game they built was named ‘Kyte’ which got more than 10,000 downloads. They got the inspiration after seeing a few kids fly kites.

Prasad comes from a humble background. His mother always wanted him to build something of his own. “Sell vegetables if you have to, but don’t settle for a job”, said his mother.

Moving on, he started his third company in 2016 called Tap Cube Studios where they were building short-form HTML5 games and distributed 300+ games in a span of 2 years.

He started his 4th company GameBlitz in 2018 which later got acquired by Loco. GameBlitz was a real-money gaming app where users can earn real money for playing games.

It was built with one single motive of letting users play multiple games in one single app. All games are casual they wanted to gratify users with real money.

When Gameblitz got merged into Loco in 2019, Prasad joined loco and has been working closely with the Founders of Loco - Anirudh Pandita and Ashwin Suresh since then.

Prasad not being a game programmer (journalism background) has successfully built three companies from scratch and is now leading the scaling operation in loco for Games and Web3.
