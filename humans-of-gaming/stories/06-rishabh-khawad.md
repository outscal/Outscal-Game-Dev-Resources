# Rishabh Khawad

**Founder · MlympiX**

> Robotics Research to Games Programming

---

A research scientist turned game developer, now founder of MlympiX, an online gaming platform, and sports clan.

Rishabh wasn’t particularly crazy about gaming back in high school. He never thought of developing games either. He went on to pursue engineering majoring in Electronics and Communication at IIIT Hyderabad simply because the other choices like medical and economic sciences didn’t appeal to him that much.

At IIIT-H, he opted for a dual bachelor’s and master’s course, thus, he got a kickstart in his research career from his third year onwards. His first work was in the Robotics research center at the university.

Next, he would go on to join TCS Research as an intern and was impressed by the scale of the work and the innovations at the company.

After graduating from IIIT, Rishabh joined TCS Research as a full-time Development System Engineer.

At the company, he got an opportunity to go to Spain as a visiting researcher. He was working on a robotic arm to help physically disabled people dress up.

However, towards the end of 2019, Rishabh felt the urge to start a company and shift his career to entrepreneurship.

After six months of research and analysis, he co-founded MlympiX, a sports community, and an online gaming platform.

Currently, MympliX has a mobile gaming app, which is a league-based sports gaming platform. It is a skill-based game that is quite fun to play. On Instagram, the company has built a sports community that connects multiple fans worldwide.

Rishabh’s career is proof that perseverance fueled with passion can help you achieve any goals in life.
