# Vyacheslav Gladyshev

**Chief Product Officer · Wargaming**

> From working in a payments company to leading the production of Wargaming Platform, which powers renowned games like World of Tanks.

---

Here's an inspiring example that shows you don't need a game dev school background to make it big in the industry.

Vyacheslav started gaming at the young age of twelve but didn't think about building a career in the gaming industry. He first earned a degree in linguistics from St Petersburg State University, Russia, followed by a degree in engineering and communication networks. After graduating, he worked as a project and product manager in various companies, from large, partly government-owned establishments to startups.

His first position was at YOTA, a Russian WiMAX startup, in 2008 where he worked as a Project Manager handling router and WiFi Center launches.

After three years, in 2011, Vyacheslav took on a new challenge as a project manager at YooMoney, Russia's largest electronic payment system. He quickly rose to Head of the Service Implementation group and ultimately became a product manager, winning the Best P2P Programme award.

Although none of these roles were directly related to game development, Vyacheslav gained valuable experience that would later help him in the gaming industry. In 2014, he interviewed for a product manager position at Wargaming, a leading video game developer and publisher in Belarus.

Initially, he was responsible for Wargaming Platform's monetization services. The industries were quite different, but his product management experience guided the team in the right direction.

Within a year, he became a program manager overseeing monetization and payments solutions for Wargaming Platform. His previous experience at YooMoney proved beneficial, demonstrating that the game dev industry values talent from diverse fields. In 2016, Vyacheslav was promoted to Development Director, managing multiple platform teams and leading key areas. In 2021, he moved to Wargaming's new Vilnius office as Director of Production, responsible for prioritization, roadmapping, and budgeting.

Recently, in March 2023, Vyacheslav was appointed Chief Product Officer (CPO) of Wargaming Platform, overseeing product management, business analysis, and delivery management. His journey shows that skills and experience trump formal education in the gaming industry. He believes that anyone can become a product manager if they're curious and care about their users.

Vyacheslav suggests talking to people, asking questions, and observing successful companies to excel in his field.
