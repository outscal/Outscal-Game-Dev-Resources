# Anshul Rustaggi

A dream journey for millions in India, [Anshul](https://www.linkedin.com/in/ACoAAAAWtzkBX6ZB9v9H-npV58cawKaSMErQhWI) lived it.

IIT, IIM, Went UK to work ‘on-site’.

Ft. [Anshul Rustaggi](https://www.linkedin.com/in/ACoAAAAWtzkBX6ZB9v9H-npV58cawKaSMErQhWI), Founder of [The Zionverse](https://www.linkedin.com/company/thezionverse/).

We were blown by the fact that Anshul pursued a degree in law when he wanted a career break after returning from the UK.

Someone else would have taken a long sabbatical but Anshul chose college life and pursued LLB.

He graduated from [Indian Institute of Technology, Delhi](https://www.linkedin.com/company/iitdelhi/) in 2001 and went to [Indian Institute of Management Bangalore](https://www.linkedin.com/company/indian-institute-of-management-bangalore/) for his master’s degree in the same year.

Left India immediately after his college to join a hedge fund in UK.

He spent a good decade working out of India and living a dream life for many Indians.

Soon, he decided to come back to India and left his cushioned job.

He took a career break and cracked the entrance exam of best law schools in India. Started pursuing LLB from [Delhi University](https://www.linkedin.com/company/delhi-university/).

This was the time when he figured out that he wanted to build something on the intersection of Games and Blockchain.

Anshul believes “What happened to iOS and Android a decade ago, we’ll start seeing that with [Unity](https://www.linkedin.com/company/unity/) soon,"

He founded [Totality Corp](https://www.linkedin.com/company/totalitycorp/) in 2017 where he started building a Web3 gaming ecosystem with foundations rooted in Indian Culture and Mythology.

Building [The Zionverse](https://www.linkedin.com/company/thezionverse/) - The first Indian Metaverse of its kind which has already sold 10K+ NFTs including Laxmi NFT, Trimurti NFT, Desi NFT which got really popular in the Indian web3 ecosystem.

Anshul firmly believes in the future of gaming, blockchain, and web3. His ambitious project is targeted to GenZ and Gen Alpha.
