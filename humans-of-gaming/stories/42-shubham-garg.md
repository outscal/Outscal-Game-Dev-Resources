# Shubham Garg

**Founder and CEO · AITA**

> Quit job amidst the pandemic and started building his own gaming studio. Ft. Shubham Garg, an electrical engineer turned game developer, now a founder.

---

Quit job amidst the pandemic and started building his own gaming studio. Ft. [Shubham Garg](https://www.linkedin.com/in/ACoAACHDkeoBhyAJM8XODme3B9_IWRHMCIMra1w), an electrical engineer turned game developer, now a founder.

7 years ago, when he graduated from [Thapar Institute Of Engineering And Technology University](https://www.linkedin.com/company/thapar-institute-of-engineering-and-technology-university/), he had no clue that he’ll end up in games.

‘‘Me and my friend [Sunny Kumar Saini](https://www.linkedin.com/in/ACoAABxlRsoB51zuS6kVp8HE1DyYxDVkEKTgYM0) were certain that we’ll have to do something of our own as we were not in the computer science branch. We had learned to code and knew that the only option we had was to find opportunities off-campus.”

When companies came for campus placement, we were surprised to see that our skills were getting more weight age than computer science degrees.”

[Shubham](https://www.linkedin.com/in/ACoAACHDkeoBhyAJM8XODme3B9_IWRHMCIMra1w) joined [Fidelity International](https://www.linkedin.com/company/fidelity-international/) as a backend developer and parallelly started learning game development to explore his interest in building games.

After working for two years in backend development, he switched to game development and started building games independently. More than a year of hustle later, he got a job offer from [RV Technologies Softwares Pvt. Ltd.](https://www.linkedin.com/company/rv-technologies-in/) to join as a Team Lead.

Spending a year there gave him the confidence to quit his job and start his own studio.

He quit his job and started building [CodeCrust](https://www.linkedin.com/company/codecrust/) in 2020.

“The biggest shift from being an employee to an employer for me was that too many things came up on my plate. Sitting hands-on to code was the last thing that I could do.

Hiring, Onboarding new employees, Training them to get on pace, rolling out salaries on time, managing finances and all other things take a toll on the founder’s time bandwidth.”

“ I was lucky enough to have found a co-founder, [Sunny](https://www.linkedin.com/in/ACoAABxlRsoB51zuS6kVp8HE1DyYxDVkEKTgYM0) who joined us in 2021. He left his comfortable MNC job to ride this roller coaster ride of uncertainty with us.”

Shubham is the epitome of hard work and decision-making.

I have been following his journey since 2015 when one of our students, [Shubham Behl](https://www.linkedin.com/in/ACoAABxJf40BEqep0w428I5bg2IRvae3BxFUwRw) was reporting to him in RV Tech.

He’s a living testimony of not only building a career in games but also providing employment opportunities to others without having a traditional degree in computer science or game engineering.
