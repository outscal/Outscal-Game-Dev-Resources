# Vaibhav Chavan

**Founder and CEO · Culttales Interactive Private Limited**

> An underdog who was laid off twice and stayed unemployed for 18 months is now building a gaming studio that many want to join.

---

Living in the city of dreams, Vaibhav started by playing video games on a black-and-white computer to become the founder of his own gaming company.

Vaibhav's story is nothing short of inspiring.

After completing his Bachelor's degree in IT, he landed a trainee game designer position at IndiaGames but was unfortunately laid off after just four months. Despite this setback, he persevered and continued to pursue his dream job.

After being jobless for 18 months, he found a programming role at a software company, only to be laid off again due to a recession.

However, he eventually landed a job at Games2win India Pvt Ltd where he learned the business of making money through games.

When we asked him, how he got a job at Games2win India Pvt Ltd, he replied

”Games2Win wasn’t hiring so I went to their office and asked them to take my interview or I will not leave” Sitansu Desai who was the game producer sat down and gave me a test. He’s still a mentor and Guru to me. He hired me and that’s when my journey in gaming started.”

Vaibhav’s stint at G2W taught him how the gaming industry worked.

He recalled. “We were shipping one game every week. The team was very small and the work was intense. Building games at that intensity taught me a lot of things and ignited the fire to build Underdogs Studio LLP.”

While working at Games2Win and Hungama, he realized that there were very few studios building high-quality games at cheap rates.

He decided to go to the other side of the table and become one of those supplier.

This gave birth to Underdogs Studio LLP.

The studio gained global recognition for its game ‘Skate Lander’, which changed the game for them.

11 years into building Underdogs studios, he recently started building Culttales Interactive Private Limited

An interactive platform with Indian stories that have multiple endings like Black Mirror Bandersnatch on Netflix.

Vaibhav Chavan's journey in the gaming industry is a testament to the power of determination and hard work. He started with humble beginnings, but with his passion and talent, he has made a name for himself in the industry.
