# Biswajit Paul

**Project Manager · Ace Turtle Services Ltd**

> “I never liked playing games in my childhood. My team always asked me to go to the game zone and play games.”

---

“I never liked playing games in my childhood. My team always asked me to go to the game zone and play games.”

Ft Biswajit Paul,

15 years in the gaming industry and Biswajit has seen the Indian gaming ecosystem evolve. Started learning game development in the third year of college from his then-senior Saurabh Sinha (Now VP - of Mayhem)

Biswajit comes from a small village where nobody had heard the term ‘game development’ when he was trying to break into the gaming industry.

He joined Gameshastra as a trainee and fell in love with game programming.

His parents had no clue what he was up to but he told his dad that he was loving what he was doing and very few people enjoy making a living.

7 years into the industry, Biswajit joined Digital Aptech as VP of Mobile games where he worked for 2 years and led multiple projects.

Next up in his career, he switched to a Product manager role where he set up a team of 33 people from scratch.

A year later, he joined Red Apple Technologies Pvt. Ltd. as a project manager where he worked on various casino games and built a cricket game for airtel.

When we asked if he ever felt that he was taking a risky bet by joining the gaming industry, he replied ”Working in the gaming industry gave me so much confidence that I never felt that things could go south.

Biswajit being from a tier 3 city in India built a successful career in games more than a decade ago.

He shared that It took him more than a month to learn a simple ‘Print statement’. It's only his commitment and consistency that brought him here.

His story is a message to all the young folks out there who think its difficult to build a career in games.
