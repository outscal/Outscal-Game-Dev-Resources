# Prashanth DG

**Producer/Co-Founder · Brewed Games**

> “If you'd asked me any time before 2020 to start a studio of my own, I would have laughed at it and said - I'm not foolish enough to do that."

---

“If you'd asked me any time before 2020 to start a studio of my own, I would have laughed at it and said - I'm not foolish enough to do that." Featuring Prashanth DG

An avid gamer since childhood, Prashanth spent days and nights playing games.

After graduating from high school, his friends started taking admission to engineering college while he was still figuring out what to do.

“I didn’t know that a career in games could exist, so never thought of it as a career option”

A friend of Prashanth always talked about game development and was going to Bangalore to visit this institute which had courses in design, and art.

They went to the Asian Institute of Design in Bangalore. Here’s how Prashanth defines his first experience there:

”It had this great studio vibe. Posters of games everywhere. It didn't look or feel like any college that I had ever seen. Dual monitor set up, people just sketching on Wacom and playing on the Xbox. I even saw one of the classes where they were teaching sketching and I thought, this is by far the most unexpectedly interesting college I had ever seen.”

The institute experience flabbergasted him and he was even more sure about making a career in games.

Prashanth pursued Professional Diploma in Game Art & Designer with his current co-founder @Yashashvi and Naveen.

After graduating, all three were placed at Atman Software where Prashanth started his professional journey as a 3D Artist.

His first step into 3D modeling, creating assets for scenes ranging from the real world to sci-fi environments. Worked

Having spent almost 4 years at Atman, he switched to real-world one where he was working on AR, and VR tech. Stepped up to a lead role within a year where he was coordinating between the design team from Germany and the dev team from India.

This role would foreshadow Prashanth's decision of switching to a project coordinator role.

In 2018, he joined Xarpie Labs as a project coordinator because he enjoyed the managerial position at the previous company.

”Now, I want to know more about working with teams, supporting them, guiding them, achieving bigger things that you can do with the team rather than what I could do just as an individual”

His experience at Xarpie Labs taught him a lot about the Product Management. He was now managing a team of designers, artists, PMs, and testers, to ensure the quality of the project.

Having spent a good amount of time in the industry, Prashanth was now ready to brew something of his own.

2021, the spirit gave birth to a new game studio called Brewed Games.

“I studied to be in games, but for whatever reason, never got a job in games. We kept waiting for someone to start a studio that would make hardcore games in India, and soon realized that we can be that someone.”
