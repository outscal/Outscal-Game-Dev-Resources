# Druhin Mukherjee

**Co-Founder · Godspeed Games**

> From being an unpaid Intern to employing 200+ people.

---

A highly skilled programmer, teacher, author, award-winning entrepreneur, angel investor, and a great human being.

Hailing from Kolkata, he has been to places across the globe and has worked in Japan, Scotland, New Zealand, Britain, and a few more places in the last 18 years.

Druhin recalled, ”The first game I played was a DOS-based Prince of Persia. That arose curiosity in me and inspired me to start my journey in game development.”

He pursued his BTech from SRM and soon relocated to Scotland for his MS.

”It was 2005 and Scotland was filled with game dev studios. Today’s big studios like Tag Games were just getting started back then.”

He was an MS student during the day and a game intern by the night.

A few years later, he and his friends were working on a game called Sculpty which got nominated for BAFTA awards in 2011 and won in 2012.

“I came back to India after finishing my MS and realized that the gaming ecosystem in India was still at a very naive stage. 15 people showed up to #IGDC that year including Rajesh Rao, Anuj Tandon, and a few more folks. I was young and realized that I’ll have to go out of India to learn more.”

He relocated to New Zealand where he was then teaching game programming to undergrads and working as a programmer in his spare time. Students taught by him are today working on amazing games in the best gaming studios across the globe.

Working on various successful games taught him that making good games and making good money out of games were two different skills.

During his stint at Lightning Fish Games, He met Ranbeer Hora (his current business partner) and decided to eventually start something of their own.

A few years down the lane, Ranbeer founded GodSpeed Games and Druhin joined in with his endeavors to build a game studio with great employee culture.

Druhin was awarded Asia 30 under 30 in 2013 and has been a great inspiration to professionals in the industry. He started his journey almost 18 years ago when games like GTA, FIFA, and God of War were not there to testify to the potential of the gaming industry.
