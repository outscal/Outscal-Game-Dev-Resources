# Avinash Bansal

**Co-Founder · Tomo Club**

> D2C founder who’s now teaching kids with games.

---

D2C founder who’s now teaching kids with games.

Featuring Avinash Bansal.

Avinash graduated from DCE in 2015 and was building ‘Leaf wearable’ Straight Outta college campus.

“You do become an average of people around you” Avinash

In our conversation with founders in the gaming industry, we asked them how they imagined their career trajectory when they were in their early 20s.

Avinash’s reply to this came as a shock.

”I had never thought of becoming an entrepreneur. My close friends led the endeavors and I tagged along.”

Avinash took exit from his first startup 2 years ago that he had built with Manik Mehta and Paras Batra. He then started solving the problem that he had noticed in hundreds of professionals and students while hiring for his first startup.

The problem was lack of ’Social and Emotional Learning skills’

Blackboard teaching dates back to the 19th century and has become completely obsolete. With advancement in technology, the traditional education system doesn’t even know that its competing with AI powered smartphones for students’ attention.

Tomo Club is bridging the gap between education and work with the help of games. Games are engaging and immersive, and Avinash’s inclination towards the gaming industry is helping hundreds of students across India develop the much needed skills of social and emotional learning.
