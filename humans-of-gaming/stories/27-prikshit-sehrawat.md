Community is contagious.

This week we got rewarded for investing on our community.

This is something that I and my team always wanted to do. Our attempt to give value to community members in the gaming industry kinda worked.

Ft. Prikshit Sehrawat

He cracked an internship at his all time fav gaming studio Electronic Arts (EA) and he’s not an Outscal Alumnus. Started learning programming back in the first year of his college, he knew that he wanted to build games.

“I started watching YouTube tutorials and learnt all by implementing. I built game environments, played around with a bunch of random models and explored more by watching Unity tutorials on YouTube. I quit game dev during pandemic and switched into android development.

In 2021, while learning android for over a year, I came across the Outscal community and started exploring their discord server. I went through a lot of resumes shared for review, talked to a lot of folks in the community and watched Outscal’s mock interview video.

All of this inspired me to get back into the gaming industry, I started learning again and at the same time came across EA’s internship opening.”

Prikshit reached out to Electronic Arts (EA)’s whole hiring team, connected with talent acquisition managers and scored an interview. That’s it!

We did zero efforts to help Prikshit break into this job role.

The community did its magic ⚡
