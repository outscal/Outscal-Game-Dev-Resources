# Kunal Khedkar

**Game Developer · Biz Technologies**

> I had access to a laptop as a kid which intrigued me a lot. I wondered how computers work. I always wanted to become an engineer so I was sure about learning programming when I was in 10th grade.

---

Kunal’s parents come from an engineering background. His elder brother was committed to programming before him.

‘‘I had access to a laptop as a kid which intrigued me a lot. I wondered how computers work. I always wanted to become an engineer so I was sure about learning programming when I was in 10th grade.

I took the first step by pursuing a diploma in computer science after 10th standard. I didn’t go with the mainstream PCM combination.

My parents supported me with this decision."

Kunal participated in various hackathons during his college years and won prizes in a few.

He developed a keen interest in augmented reality during the lock down.

Learning AR led him to the Unity gaming engine and then to game development.

He eventually found himself more interested in building games.

Kunal started learning game dev from YouTube tutorials and short courses on Udemy.

He was about to graduate from college so he started planning higher education in game development.

While hunting for MS, he came across our launchpad scholarship.

He joined our launchpad clan and eventually our flagship program in game development.

We need more Kunals in the gaming industry. But to achieve that we’ll need more parents like these.
