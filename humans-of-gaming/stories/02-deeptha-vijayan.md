28 years in the gaming industry X Ft. Deeptha Vijayan

From Floppy Discs and CD-ROMS to Steam Accounts

Starting in 1994, she wanted to explore her interests in the creative industry but accidentally got into engineering. She started by converting puzzle games and board games into virtual games.

Deeptha’s team created the first puzzle game on CD-ROM for kids in India. She dedicated 16 years of her life to Western Outdoor Interactive (WOI) - A GEE Company from 1994 to 2010.

Her professional journey from 2010 to 2022 has been filled with gaming studios that are dream work spaces for millions of people.

She joined Sumo India Studios as Head of the Studio in 2010 where she was managing the whole studio and also took care of the talent division.

Her passion for games gave her the courage to quit her full-time job and build something of her own. She found a different kind of happiness while building her first startup.

2 years of grind as a founder helped her explore more interesting opportunities in game production. Deeptha was now working as Head of Production in Ruby Seven Studios, Inc..

With more than 20 years of experience, she was now working on real money games and spearheading the production of their own IP at Ruby Seven Studios, Inc..

Up next in her career was a short stint at Cympl Studios where she got to work on Hindu mythological games.

Deeptha returned to Sumo Digital Ltd in 2018 as Studio Director. ( 8 years after her previous job here) She was now working on super interesting game projects.

Lockdown and COVID downturn brought new job offers for her and she joined JetSynthesys Pvt. Ltd. in 2020 during peak covid months which shows how the right skills can never be impacted by any disaster in the world.

This is not it!

Deeptha has been recently working as the Director of Business Operations at Zynga.

When we asked her if she was ever fascinated by MAANG companies, her prompt answer was a blunt ‘no’!

“If you love games and want to work in a creative field, Sky is the limit for you in the gaming industry.” - Deeptha

I am uber-inspired by her journey!

Deeptha’s journey is clear instance of how constant hard work and hunger to learn can make you last and stay relevant in the same industry for the longest time.

Reinventing oneself even in the same field is just as important as starting something new.
